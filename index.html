Our Story

Founded by renowned wellness expert Isabella Montrose, Luxury Wellness was born from a vision to create Calgary's most opulent and transformative spa experience.

With over two decades of experience in luxury hospitality and wellness, Isabella recognized the need for a sanctuary where discerning clients could escape and indulge in the ultimate wellness journey.

Our philosophy combines timeless spa traditions with innovative wellness technologies in an environment of uncompromising luxury.

Our Philosophy

To provide an extraordinary wellness experience that transcends traditional spa services, creating lasting transformation through luxury, innovation, and personalized care.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you book appointments, sign up for our newsletter, or contact us for services. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and health-related information necessary for treatments.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, and ensure your safety and comfort during treatments. We may also use your information to send you promotional materials about our services.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted partners who assist us in operating our business, conducting services, or serving you.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
