About Luxury Wellness

Pioneering luxury wellness since 2010

Our Story

Founded by renowned wellness expert Isabella Montrose, Luxury Wellness was born from a vision to create the world's most opulent and transformative spa experience.

With over two decades of experience in luxury hospitality and wellness, Isabella recognized the need for a sanctuary where discerning clients could escape and indulge in the ultimate wellness journey.

Our philosophy combines timeless spa traditions with innovative wellness technologies in an environment of uncompromising luxury.

Our Philosophy

To provide an extraordinary wellness experience that transcends traditional spa services, creating lasting transformation through luxury, innovation, and personalized care.

  • Commitment to excellence and innovation
  • Sustainable luxury practices
